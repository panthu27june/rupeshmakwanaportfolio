About Me

Rupesh Makwana is an Indian ultra-endurance runner and coach, renowned for his remarkable achievements in long-distance running. Born on March 11, 1993, in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, he has pushed the limits of human endurance.

In August 2023, he achieved the world record for the longest nonstop marathon by an individual (male), running 375 km over 75 hours in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India.

In 2023, he earned a Guinness World Record for the fastest time to travel the Indian Golden Quadrilateral on foot, covering 6,000 km in 88 days.

Rupesh is now a motivational figure and coach, sharing ultra-endurance tips, training guidance, and mental fitness strategies to inspire athletes globally.
